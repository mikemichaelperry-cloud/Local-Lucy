## What Was Done in This Session

### 1. Medical & Veterinary Evidence Routing
**Commit:** `077276cd`

**Problem:** Medical and veterinary queries were routing to AUGMENTED (general knowledge) instead of EVIDENCE (trusted sources). The model could give dangerous advice.

**Solution:**
- Added `EVIDENCE` route with `trusted` provider and domain allowlists
- Medical allowlist: cochranelibrary.com, pubmed, jama, nejm, medlineplus, dailymed, thelancet
- Veterinary allowlist: merckvetmanual.com, vcahospitals.com, avma.org, aspca.org, petmd.com, aaha.org
- 3-tier veterinary detection:
  1. Specific animals (dog, cat, horse, bird, guinea pig, bearded dragon, etc.) → immediate vet context
  2. General terms (pet, animal, livestock) → require health co-occurrence
  3. Veterinary procedures/sources (spay, heartworm, merck vet) → immediate trigger
- Word-boundary regex prevents false positives ("cat" in "medication")
- `medical_vet_safety_override` guard forces EVIDENCE regardless of embedding prediction
- Test suite: `test_medical_evidence_routing.py` (14 tests, 32 subtests, all pass)
